### AquaFaste Positioning

**Unique Moat'lar (sadece AquaFaste'te):**
1. **Hydration Score (0-100)** — günlük performansı tek sayıyla özetleyen skor. Hiçbir rakipte yok
2. **EFSA-based kişisel hedef** — bilimsel hesaplama (30 mL/kg). Rakipler generic "8 bardak" kullanıyor
3. **Caffeine tracking** — su + kafein birlikte takip. Sadece WaterMinder'da var (ama ayrı)
4. **Zero 3rd party** — tamamen Apple native. Privacy advantage

**Positioning Statement:**
> For health-conscious adults who want to understand their hydration, AquaFaste is the only water tracker that gives you a personal Hydration Score — a daily rating from 0 to 100 based on your drinking consistency, timing, and goal completion. Unlike Waterllama or WaterMinder, AquaFaste combines science-based goals with a single score that makes hydration as trackable as your step count.
