AquaFaste is an honest hydration tracker for people who care about their health — not apps that nag you with ads. No ads. No gimmicks. Just clean, science-based water tracking with HealthKit sync.

PERSONALIZED DAILY GOAL
Your daily target is calculated using the EFSA-aligned formula — 30 ml per kg body weight, adjusted for your activity level. Choose from five levels (Sedentary to Very Active) or set a custom goal.

12 DRINK TYPES WITH HYDRATION RATIOS
Not all drinks hydrate equally. AquaFaste tracks Water, Coffee, Tea, Juice, Milk, Soda, Sparkling Water, Coconut Water, Smoothie, Soup, Beer, and Wine — each with a science-based hydration ratio. Milk hydrates 1.5× better than water. Coffee counts at 85%. Every sip is weighted for real hydration.

CAFFEINE TRACKING
See your daily caffeine total at a glance and get a warning when you pass 400 mg — the FDA-recommended daily limit.

HEALTHKIT SYNC
Every water log syncs to Apple Health automatically. AquaFaste reads your body weight from HealthKit to keep your daily goal accurate.

SMART REMINDERS
Configurable reminders during your waking hours. Morning motivation, evening summary, streak protection — all respecting your quiet hours. Smart timing suppresses reminders for 30 minutes after you log.

STREAKS & ACHIEVEMENTS
Build daily streaks, unlock 12 achievement badges, and celebrate milestones with confetti. Streak freeze protects your progress on tough days.

DETAILED STATISTICS

• Personalized hydration score (0-100)
