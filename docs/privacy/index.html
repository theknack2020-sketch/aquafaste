Privacy Policy

Effective: March 22, 2025

Overview

AquaFaste ("we", "our", "the app") is a hydration tracking app developed by The Knack. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Health Data: AquaFaste reads your body weight (with your permission) from Apple Health to calculate your personalized daily hydration goal. We write your water intake data to Apple Health so it's available alongside your other health data.

Local Data: Your hydration logs, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data is not transmitted to any server.

No Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs. We do not track your behavior, location, or usage patterns.

No Advertising: We do not display ads and do not share any data with advertisers.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that store your personal data. If you delete the app, all local data is removed.

Subscriptions

AquaFaste offers optional premium subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. Purchase history and billing are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

AquaFaste does not integrate with any third-party services other than Apple HealthKit (with your explicit permission) and Apple StoreKit (for in-app purchases).

Children's Privacy

AquaFaste does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
