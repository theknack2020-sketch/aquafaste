## 3. Description — English (4000 chars max)

```
AquaFaste is an honest hydration tracker built for people who care about their health — not for apps that nag you with ads. No ads. No gimmicks. Just clean, science-based water tracking with HealthKit sync.

SMART DAILY GOAL
Your daily water target is calculated from your body weight and activity level using the IOM/EFSA research-backed formula (weight × 35 ml × activity multiplier). Choose from five activity levels — Sedentary to Very Active — or set a custom goal. Your body, your number.

12 DRINK TYPES WITH HYDRATION RATIOS
Not all drinks hydrate equally. AquaFaste tracks Water, Coffee, Tea, Juice, Milk, Soda, Sparkling Water, Coconut Water, Smoothie, Soup, Beer, and Wine — each with a science-based hydration ratio. Milk hydrates 1.5× better than water. Beer counts at 40%. Coffee at 85%. Every sip is weighted for real hydration.

CAFFEINE TRACKING
AquaFaste automatically calculates caffeine intake from your logged drinks. See your daily caffeine total at a glance, and get a warning when you pass 400 mg — the FDA-recommended daily limit.

HEALTHKIT SYNC
Every water log syncs to Apple Health automatically. AquaFaste can also read your body weight from HealthKit to keep your daily goal accurate without manual entry.

SMART REMINDERS
Configurable reminders every 1–3 hours during your waking hours. Morning motivation, evening summary, streak protection, and refill reminders — all respecting your quiet hours. Smart timing suppresses reminders for 30 minutes after you log a drink, so you're never nagged right after drinking.

STREAKS & INSIGHTS
Build daily streaks and watch your consistency grow. AquaFaste generates personalized insights: weekday vs. weekend patterns, peak hydration hour, drink type breakdown, goal achievement rate, and average daily caffeine. The longer you track, the smarter your insights become.

DETAILED STATISTICS
• Weekly bar charts with goal line
• Week-over-week comparison
• Monthly calendar heatmap
• Time-of-day area chart
• Drink type donut chart with percentages
• Current streak, best streak, all-time total

FAVORITES & QUICK ADD
Save your go-to drinks as favorites for one-tap logging. Three customizable cup size buttons (Small, Medium, Large) plus custom amount entry. Recent drinks appear for instant re-logging.

BEAUTIFUL DESIGN
Animated circular progress ring with real-time wave fill. Confetti celebration when you hit your goal. Splash effects on every log. Four color themes — Ocean, Forest, Berry, and Mono. Smooth number animations and haptic feedback make every interaction feel satisfying.

TIMELINE & HISTORY
See today's drinks in a visual timeline. Edit or delete past logs. View the last three days of history with full drink details. Export your entire history as CSV for backup or analysis.

UNDO & EDIT
Made a mistake? Undo your last drink with one tap, or long-press any log to edit the amount or drink type.

PRIVACY FIRST
AquaFaste stores all data locally on your device using SwiftData. No account required. No analytics. No tracking. Your hydration data is yours alone.

PREMIUM (OPTIONAL)
Free users get full water tracking, daily goals, and HealthKit sync. Premium unlocks custom drinks, smart reminders, detailed stats, data export, themes, and streak recovery. Available as monthly, yearly, or lifetime purchase.

Built with SwiftUI for iOS 17+. Designed to be the last water tracker you'll ever need.
```

### HealthKit Data Details

| Data Type        | Read | Write | Purpose                                      |
|------------------|------|-------|----------------------------------------------|
| Dietary Water    | ✅   | ✅    | Sync water intake logs to/from Apple Health   |
| Body Mass        | ✅   | ❌    | Calculate personalized daily hydration goal   |

### Privacy Practices

- **No data collected** — all hydration data is stored locally on device (SwiftData)
- **No account required** — no email, no sign-up, no user identifier
- **No analytics or tracking SDKs** — zero third-party analytics
- **No advertising** — no ad SDKs, no ad identifiers
- **No server communication** — app operates 100% offline (except StoreKit for purchases)
- **HealthKit data** is used solely for water intake sync and weight-based goal calculation
- **Data deletion** — users can reset all data from Settings → Danger Zone
