FAQ

How is my daily goal calculated?

Your goal is based on your body weight (kg) × 35ml × activity multiplier. For a 70kg moderately active person, that's about 2,940ml per day.

What are hydration ratios?

Not all drinks hydrate equally. Coffee counts as ~85% water due to mild diuretic effects, while milk actually hydrates better than water (150%). Our ratios are based on published hydration research.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → tap "Restore Purchases" on the premium page. Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

Does AquaFaste work with Apple Health?

Yes! AquaFaste writes your water intake to Apple Health and can read your body weight to personalize your goal. Grant permissions when prompted.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → AquaFaste → Cancel.
